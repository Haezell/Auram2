ABOUT TANZANIA WILDLIFE

Tanzania has 38% of its territory allocated as protected wildlife areas, thus has a diverse range of national parks and game reserves to suit a variety of interests and travel preferences. There are three main ‘safari circuits’ in Tanzania: the famous Northern Circuit, which includes the Serengeti – home of the world’s greatest migration, Ngorongoro Crater – the world’s largest caldera. A calmer Southern Circuit, which provides a secluded wilderness experience away from the people, Ruaha National Park, Selous Game Reserve, Mikumi, Udzungwa. The Western Circuit includes Mahale Park, Gombe Stream National Park and Rubondo Island Parks provides amazing views of chimpanzee families. Jane Goodall Chimpanzee Project brought a lot of attention to the Tanzania primates
We can propose the best parks and accommodations for your preferences, ranging from romantic camps in off-the-beaten-path areas of the nation to family-friendly resorts.

Tanzania the land of the most stunning landscapes, exceptional wildlife experiences and luxurious safari camps and lodges in Africa. Tanzania is the home of one of the greatest spectacles in the world – The Great Serengeti Migration.
Auram Safaris specializes in planning thrilling wildlife excursions in Tanzania. Our safaris are popular amongst our clients, who return enthusiastically recommend our safaris to their friends and rave about us to the agents which sent them to us. People return year after year to see Tanzania’s amazing wild animals and gorgeous natural surroundings. The country features an extremely wide and distinct diversity of animals, birds, and flora and fauna because to its variable temperature, geography, and vegetation.
